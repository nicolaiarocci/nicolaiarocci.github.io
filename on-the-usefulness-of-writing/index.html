On the usefulness of writing

December 2, 2025

I think of it [the usefulness of writing] like breathing but for ideas. We do so much reading all day—there should be a natural balance with producing words too. Inhale, exhale, inhale, exhale…

Joe Boudreau in On 10 Years of Writing a Blog Nobody Reads, an article I agree 100% with.
