No Soy Tua

November 25, 2025

I was walking early this morning, as usual, when I came across an unusual advertising sign. It was in Spanish, with only text, red background and black letters.

I don’t comprehend Spanish well enough to read it, so I photographed it and sent it to Serena, who promptly translated it for me.

I AM NOT YOURS

  1. YOU DON’T ABUSE ME, NOT EVEN ONLINE
  2. YOU DON’T CONTROL WHAT I DO
  3. YOU DON’T CONTROL HOW MUCH I SPEND
  4. YOU DON’T CONTROL WHO I SEE • YOU DON’T BLACKMAIL ME
  5. YOU DON’T HARASS ME
  6. YOU DON’T TOUCH ME IF I DON’T WANT TO
  7. YOU DON’T HIT ME
  8. YOU DON’T KILL ME.

When I got back, I traced it to the source.
